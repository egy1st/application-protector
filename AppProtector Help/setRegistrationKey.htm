setRegistrationKey Function

setRegistrationKey Function enables you to set your purchased serial number, to get a complete use of your software. if you deployed an application that use Dc AppProtector without setting your registration key, this will result in an annoying popup screen.

Syntax:

Public Sub setRegistrationKey(ByVal myKey As String)

Example:

oProtection.setRegistrationKey("0000-000-000-000-0000")

 

Note:

This function should be used immediately after your initiate your instance of DC AppProtector

Example:

Dim MyProtection As New DynamicComponents.AppProtector()

MyProtection.setRegistrationKey("0000-000-000-000-0000") ' replace with your purchased serial number once you get one

MyProtection.SetAlgorithms(5555, 22, 99)

MyProtection.ShowAuthor()
